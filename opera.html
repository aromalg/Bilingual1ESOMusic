What is Opera?

Sydney Opera House

Opera is a special kind of musical show that tells a story with singing, acting, and music. Here are some of its main characteristics:

  • Aria is a solo song that shows strong emotions. It's often sung with the orchestra and lets the characters share their feelings.
  • Recitativo is a mix of talking and singing that moves the story forward in a natural way, connecting the emotional arias with regular speech.
  • Leitmotif is a repeating musical theme linked to characters or feelings, making the story more interesting and connected.

As you can see, opera is a mix of all these things, creating a beautiful and captivating performance with music, stories, and acting.
