PACO DE LUCÍA, SPANISH GUITAR PLAYER

Paco de Lucía was a fantastic guitar player from Spain. He was born on December 21, 1947, in Algeciras. His full name was Francisco Sánchez Gómez.

He loved playing the guitar since he was a little kid. He played a special kind of music called flamenco. But what made him really cool was that he mixed flamenco with other types of music like jazz and Latin American music.

Paco de Lucía made many songs, but some of his most famous ones are "Entre Dos Aguas," "Almoraima," and "Friday Night in San Francisco." In this last one, he played with other great guitar players.

Paco de Lucía won important music awards, like the Grammy Awards. People from all around the world loved his music.
